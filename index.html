Wall of Art sells exclusive posters created by our artists, photographers, graphic designers and illustrators' original works. We print our items on an exclusive 200 gram matte premium paper to recreate the right feeling and color reproduction. Our prints are available in several sizes from 21x30cm up to 70x100. We also sell some selected of our artworks in large format up to 100x140cm. With us you can find motifs that are only sold via Wall of Art. We send our prints and items to the whole world and offer free shipping and fast deliveries in Sweden. We also sell frames for our posters. We have ekram, vitram and svartram that can be combined according to which works of art you choose.
